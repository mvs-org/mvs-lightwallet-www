Light Wallet Guidelines & FAQs

1. How is the safety of light wallets guaranteed?

Information from the private key file never leaves the users’ browser. All transactions are signed within the browser, and only signed transactions will be broadcasted to the network.

2. My block height is displayed as “0” when I log into the wallet. What do I do?

Select the “re-sync” option in the upper right corner of the wallet.

3. I have no assets when I log into the wallet. What do I do?

There are two possible reasons. One, your wallet might not be fully synced; please select the “re-sync” option in the upper right corner of the wallet. The other is that the wallet you have logged into does not contain any assets. Please check that you have not imported an erroneous private key.

4. I’ve lost my keystore containing my private key file. What do I do?

You can generate a new keystore through the “create file through mnemonic” function. As your wallet may be compromised due to the lost private key file, we recommend transferring your assets out of the wallet as soon as possible.

5. I’ve lost the login details for my light wallet. What do I do?

You can generate a new keystore through the “create file through mnemonic” function and reset your login password, then use the new private key file and password to log into the light wallet.

6. Can others log into my light wallet if they know my password?

The only way to log into the wallet is through the private key file. If others know your wallet’s password but do not have access to your private key file, they will still be unable to log into your light wallet account.

7. I am automatically logged into my wallet each time I open it. What if I feel that this is unsafe?

You may select the “Logout” option in the upper right corner of the wallet each time you close the wallet. When you reopen it, the wallet will not log you in automatically. To ensure the safety of your assets, we recommend logging out each time once you have finished using the wallet.

8. If I manage my assets using a different wallet, will the light wallet sync to reflect the changes?

All information displayed in the light wallet is taken from the blockchain. Hence, your transaction records left by managing your assets using a different wallet will also be reflected in the light wallet.

9. When sending transaction, errors "couldn't create transaction, please input again" showing out ?

This error shows out due to not enough ETP to pay for mining fee, please make sure you have at least 0.0001ETP for mining fee. (Eg: if you total have 10 ETP, you could only send out 9.9999ETP)
